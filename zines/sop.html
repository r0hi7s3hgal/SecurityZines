Same Origin Policy


SOP in Simple Terms


This Zine discusses about the Same Origin Policy which is a browser security model to protect user data from malicious sites/scripts by restriction access for cross-site interaction. It explains in and out of SOP in a very casual way by showing them in the graphics for better retention.

I have covered: What is SOP ? What are Origins ? World without SOP. World with SOP. By Passing SOP CORS Headers Access-Control-Allow-Origin Access-Control-Allow-Credentials Domain Lowering Security Issues with Domain lowering.
